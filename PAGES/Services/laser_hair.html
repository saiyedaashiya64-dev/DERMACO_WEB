What is Laser Hair Reduction?

Laser Hair Reduction is an advanced aesthetic treatment designed to reduce unwanted body hair by targeting hair follicles using controlled laser energy. It is a safe and effective alternative to shaving, waxing, or threading.

At DermaCo., Laser Hair Reduction treatments are dermatologist-led and performed using advanced laser technology suitable for Indian skin types. Over multiple sessions, the treatment helps convert thick, dark hair into finer, lighter hair.

Laser Hair Reduction safely reduces unwanted hair using advanced laser technology under expert supervision.

How Laser Hair Reduction Works

Laser Hair Reduction works by directing laser energy toward the melanin pigment present in hair follicles. The laser damages the follicle without harming the surrounding skin.

Over multiple sessions, hair growth slows down significantly, and regrowth becomes finer and lighter.

  • Targets hair follicle pigment
  • Does not damage skin
  • Reduces hair growth gradually

FAQs About Laser Hair Reduction at DermaCo.'s Clinic

Is Laser Hair Reduction safe? +
Yes, Laser Hair Reduction is a safe and clinically approved procedure when performed under expert supervision. At DermaCo.'s Clinic, we use advanced laser technology suitable for Indian skin types to ensure safety and effective results.
Does Laser Hair Reduction permanently remove hair? +
Laser Hair Reduction significantly reduces hair growth. While it may not permanently remove all hair, it makes the hair finer, lighter, and slower-growing over multiple sessions, resulting in long-lasting hair reduction.
How does Laser Hair Reduction work? +
Laser Hair Reduction works by directing laser energy toward the melanin pigment in hair follicles. The laser damages the follicle without affecting the surrounding skin, thereby slowing down future hair growth safely and effectively.
How many sessions are required for Laser Hair Reduction? +
The number of sessions depends on factors such as the treatment area, hair thickness, hair growth cycle, and individual response. Most individuals require multiple sessions for optimal and long-lasting results.
Is Laser Hair Reduction painful? +
Laser Hair Reduction is generally well tolerated. You may experience mild warmth or a slight tingling sensation during the procedure. Advanced cooling technology is used to ensure maximum comfort during treatment.
Is there any downtime after Laser Hair Reduction? +
There is minimal to no downtime after Laser Hair Reduction. Mild redness or sensitivity may occur temporarily and typically subsides within a short period.
Can Laser Hair Reduction be done on all body areas? +
Yes, Laser Hair Reduction can be performed on most body areas, including the face, underarms, arms, legs, chest, back, and bikini area, depending on individual suitability.
