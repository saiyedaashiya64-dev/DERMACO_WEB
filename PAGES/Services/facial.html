Types of Facial Treatments

Facial treatments are available in different forms, each designed to address specific skin concerns. At DermaCo., our dermatologists recommend facial treatments based on your skin type, condition, and treatment goals to ensure safe and effective results.

HydraFacial

HydraFacial is an advanced multi-step facial treatment that deeply cleanses, exfoliates, and hydrates the skin using a specialized medical-grade device. The treatment removes dead skin cells and impurities while infusing nourishing serums into the skin.

It is suitable for all skin types and helps improve skin texture, reduce fine lines, minimize pores, and enhance overall skin radiance with immediate visible results.

Aqua Facial

Aqua Facial is a gentle, water-based treatment that focuses on cleansing, exfoliating, and deeply hydrating the skin. It uses hydrating solutions to remove dirt, excess oil, and surface impurities without causing irritation.

This facial is ideal for dry, sensitive, or dehydrated skin and helps restore moisture balance while leaving the skin soft, fresh, and glowing.

Oxygen Facial

Oxygen Facial delivers a stream of pressurized oxygen enriched with vitamins and antioxidants to rejuvenate and energize the skin. This treatment improves blood circulation and boosts collagen production, resulting in brighter and healthier-looking skin.

It is especially beneficial for dull, tired, or stressed skin and is a popular choice before special occasions due to its instant glow effect.
