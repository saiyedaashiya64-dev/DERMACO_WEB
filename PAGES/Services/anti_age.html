Why Skin Ageing Happens

Skin ageing is a natural biological process that occurs due to a gradual decline in collagen and elastin, the proteins responsible for skin firmness and elasticity. As their production slows down, the skin becomes thinner, less firm, and more prone to wrinkles and sagging.

External factors play a major role in accelerating ageing. Prolonged sun exposure damages collagen fibers, leading to premature wrinkles and pigmentation. Pollution, smoking, stress, and poor lifestyle habits further weaken the skinâ€™s natural repair mechanism.

Hormonal changes, especially during the late 30s, menopause, or due to medical conditions, can cause dryness, loss of elasticity, and dullness. Reduced cell renewal also slows down skin healing and regeneration.

Understanding the cause of ageing helps dermatologists design the right treatment plan. At Derma Co, treatments target both internal and external ageing factors to slow down visible ageing and protect long-term skin health.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is anti-ageing treatment? +
Anti-ageing treatments help reduce fine lines, wrinkles, sagging, and improve overall skin firmness and texture using dermatologist-led medical procedures.
At what age should anti-ageing treatments be started? +
Preventive anti-ageing treatments can be started in the late 20s or early 30s, depending on skin condition, lifestyle, and sun exposure.
Are anti-ageing treatments safe? +
Yes. At Derma Co, all anti-ageing treatments are dermatologist-supervised and performed using FDA-approved technology to ensure safety and effectiveness.
How many sessions are required to see results? +
The number of sessions depends on the treatment type and skin condition. Some treatments show visible results in a few sessions, while others require a structured plan.
Is there any downtime after treatment? +
Most anti-ageing treatments have minimal to no downtime. Mild redness or sensitivity may occur and usually settles quickly.
Can anti-ageing treatments be combined? +
Yes. Dermatologists often recommend combining treatments like peels, lasers, or injectables for enhanced and long-lasting results.
